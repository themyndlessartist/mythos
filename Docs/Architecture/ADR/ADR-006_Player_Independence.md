# ADR-006 — Player Independence

ADR number: ADR-006

Title: Player Independence

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

The player is not inherently the center of the world or the sole driver of events.

## Decision

The framework will support world progression, story progression, and NPC activity that can occur independently of player action.

## Consequences

Systems must not assume that the player initiates, observes, or resolves every important world event.

## Related systems

Event Framework, Time Framework, NPC Framework, World History
