# ADR-009 — Broad Ownership

ADR number: ADR-009

Title: Broad Ownership

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Long-term Mythos goals include ownership of property, businesses, organizations, transport, and other assets.

## Decision

The framework will support broad ownership concepts through abstract, setting-independent ownership models when approved specifications require them.

## Consequences

Ownership systems must avoid setting-specific assumptions and must support both player and NPC ownership where approved.

## Related systems

Entity Framework, Economy Framework, Organization Framework, Property Systems
