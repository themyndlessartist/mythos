# ADR-016 — NPC Goals and Ambitions

ADR number: ADR-016

Title: NPC Goals and Ambitions

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Named NPCs should have motivations that make them feel like participants in the world.

## Decision

The framework will support NPC goals and ambitions at an appropriate simulation depth, subject to approved NPC specifications.

## Consequences

NPC systems must account for scalable planning depth without committing the framework to one final AI implementation.
