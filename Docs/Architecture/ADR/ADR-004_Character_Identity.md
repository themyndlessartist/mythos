# ADR-004 — Character Identity

ADR number: ADR-004

Title: Character Identity

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Players and named NPCs require identities that support persistence, relationships, roles, and world history.

## Decision

The framework will treat character identity as persistent and meaningful, independent of any single profession, combat role, or setting.

## Consequences

Character systems must preserve identity across role changes, relationships, schedules, history, ownership, and approved simulation behavior.

## Related systems

Character Framework, NPC Framework, Relationship Framework, World History
