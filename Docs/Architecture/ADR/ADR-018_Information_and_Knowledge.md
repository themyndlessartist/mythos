# ADR-018 — Information and Knowledge

ADR number: ADR-018

Title: Information and Knowledge

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Characters and systems may have different knowledge than the objective world state.

## Decision

The framework will distinguish reality, knowledge, and belief where approved systems require information modeling.

## Consequences

Future information systems must define how facts are discovered, remembered, shared, hidden, distorted, or forgotten.

Information Systems, Character Framework, NPC Framework, Event Framework
