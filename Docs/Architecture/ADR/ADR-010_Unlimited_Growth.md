# ADR-010 — Unlimited Growth

ADR number: ADR-010

Title: Unlimited Growth

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Mythos should avoid arbitrary progression limits where long-term growth can be governed by time, effort, and opportunity.

## Decision

The framework will support long-term character growth without assuming fixed class or profession caps in shared architecture.

## Consequences

Progression specifications must define pacing and constraints without hard-coding a single title's progression model into the framework.

## Related systems

Character Framework, Gameplay Frameworks, Profession Systems, Skill Systems
