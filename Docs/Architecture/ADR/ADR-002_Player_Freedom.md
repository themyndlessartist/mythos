# ADR-002 — Player Freedom

ADR number: ADR-002

Title: Player Freedom

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Mythos is intended to support player-defined success rather than a single mandatory victory condition.

## Decision

The framework will support multiple valid player paths and avoid assuming one central progression route in shared systems.

## Consequences

Gameplay systems must remain flexible enough to support exploration, professions, organizations, wealth, story participation, story avoidance, and other approved paths.

## Related systems

Character Framework, Gameplay Frameworks, Organization Framework, Economy Framework
