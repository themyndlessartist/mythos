# ADR-001 — Living World

ADR number: ADR-001

Title: Living World

Version: 0.1

Status: Approved

Owner: Mythos Executive Development

Date: July 2026

## Context

Mythos titles are intended to present worlds that exist before the player arrives and continue without requiring player participation.

## Decision

The Mythos Framework will treat the world as persistent and active. World systems may progress independently of the player according to approved simulation specifications.

## Consequences

Systems must be designed for persistent state, simulation updates, historical continuity, and events that can occur without player involvement.

## Related systems

Time Framework, Event Framework, Region Framework, NPC Framework, World History
